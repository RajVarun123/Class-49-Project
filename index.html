Interesting Facts about turtles

  1. They hide in their shells when they feel threatened
  2. They are slow in land and fast in water because of their limb structure
  3. They are endangered so dont kill any of them

Interesting Facts about humming birds

  • They drink nectar using their long beak
  • They flap their wings 80 times per second to fly
  • The size of a full grown humming bird is between 3 to 4 inches
